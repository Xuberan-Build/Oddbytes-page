Questions and Notes

Leadership Discussion

  • Who set the vision & mission for Oddbytes?
  • What is the vision for oddbytes?
  • How do you champion and lead the vision of the organization?
  • What hurdles/challenges is Oddbytes currently facing, and how are you looking to overcome them?
  • What enables you to thrive in the organization?
  • What in your mind would make me the most valuable long term hire?
  • Given my skills and background, What problems do you think I can help you solve?
  • What does success look like for the next generation of oddbytes employees?

Product Discussion

  • How does the mission and vision inform our products?
  • Can we discuss Lightbox, New Tab, and Push Ad in more detail?
  • How have you explored leveraging your existing ad tech further?
    • Can you use your ad tech on other platforms? Reposition the products for additional use cases?
  • How has 'Eureka' progressed?
  • What new products/projects are currently in production?
  • How often do you roll out new projects?
    • What does the highlevel process look like from idea to market?
  • Is there a thesis or theory about oddbytes approach to product?
  • What role does innovation play in Oddbytes' product strategy?

Customer Discussion

  • Who does Oddbytes connect with most effectively, and how do we want to exchange value with them?
  • Who do we currently serve, and who do we aspire to serve in the future?
  • What specific value are we striving to deliver to the market?
  • What does Oddbytes want to be known for in the market?
  • How does Oddbytes measure success in its relationships with clients and partners?
  • What are the biggest opportunities for growth that Oddbytes is currently pursuing?
